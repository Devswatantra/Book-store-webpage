Wings of fire

          by Arun Tiwari

Every common man who by his sheer grit and hard work
achieves success should share his story with the rest for
they may find Inspiration and strength to go on,in his
story.The 'wings of Fire' is one such autography by
visionary scientist Dr APJ Abdul Kalam,who from very
humble begginnings rose to be the president of india.The
book is fill of insight,personal moments and life
experiences of Dr.Kalam.It gives us an unferstanding on
his journey of succes.

The 3 mistakes of my life

by Chetan Bhagat

The 3 mistakes of my life is the third novel written by
eminent Indian Author Cheten Bhagat. Based on cricket
business and religion,the novel is set against the
backdrop of beautiful city Ahemdabad.Revolving around
three young Indian boys Oml,Ishaan and Govind,the
book goes on to narrate how the three are trying their
best to make ends meet in the city. Based on real events,
the book stars with dramatic twist,where Bhagat is
reading an e-mail sent by young person Govind.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter's life is miserable.His power are dead and
